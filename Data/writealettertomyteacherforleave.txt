Here's a sample letter you can use as a template:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Section]
[Your School Name]
[Date]

Respected [Teacher's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from school for the following reason(s):

[State the reason for leave, e.g. personal emergency, family obligation, illness, etc.]

I would like to take leave from [Date] to [Date] and return to school on [Date]. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to complete all my pending assignments and catch up on any work that I may miss during my absence.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could grant me permission for this leave. If there is any additional information required or if there are any concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me or my parents.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

---

Alternatively, you can also write a shorter and more concise version:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Section]
[Your School Name]
[Date]

Respected [Teacher's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from school from [Date] to [Date] due to [reason]. I will make sure to complete all my pending assignments and catch up on any work that I may miss.

Please let me know if this is acceptable.

Thank you.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
